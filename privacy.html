Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Summary: Mini Games Arcade is designed for children and families. We collect minimal data (only display name and scores for leaderboards). The mobile app contains no advertising, tracking, or data collection beyond what you choose to submit.

Information We Collect

When you use Mini Games Arcade, we may collect:

  • Display Name: The name you enter when submitting a high score (optional, you can use "Anonymous")
  • Game Scores: Your scores and the difficulty level played

We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or any personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Display your scores on public leaderboards
  • Improve game performance and user experience

No Advertising or Tracking

The Mini Games Arcade mobile app does not contain any advertising. We do not use any advertising frameworks, tracking technologies, or analytics that collect personal information. We do not share any data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

Your game scores are stored on Supabase, a secure cloud database. We retain scores indefinitely to maintain the leaderboards. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Children's Privacy

Mini Games Arcade is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The only data collected (display name and score) does not identify individuals.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Play without submitting scores (no data collected)
  • Use "Anonymous" as your display name
  • Request deletion of your scores
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request data deletion, please contact us at:
