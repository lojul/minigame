Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Summary: Mini Games Arcade collects minimal data. We only store your chosen display name and game scores on our leaderboards. We use ads to keep the app free.

Information We Collect

When you use Mini Games Arcade, we may collect:

  • Display Name: The name you enter when submitting a high score (optional, you can use "Anonymous")
  • Game Scores: Your scores and the difficulty level played
  • Device Information: Basic device type for analytics (collected by our ad partners)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Display your scores on public leaderboards
  • Show relevant advertisements to support the free app
  • Improve game performance and user experience

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to show personalized ads. This includes:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS)
  • General location (country/region)
  • App usage data

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings or when prompted by the app.

Data Storage

Your game scores are stored on Supabase, a secure cloud database. We retain scores indefinitely to maintain the leaderboards. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Children's Privacy

Mini Games Arcade is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The only data collected (display name and score) does not identify individuals.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Play without submitting scores (no data collected)
  • Use "Anonymous" as your display name
  • Request deletion of your scores
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request data deletion, please contact us at:

privacy@minigamesarcade.app
